## Operations

- list_surveys: GET /surveys
- create_survey: POST /surveys
- get_survey: GET /surveys/{survey_id}
- list_participants: GET /surveys/{survey_id}/participants
- add_participants: POST /surveys/{survey_id}/participants
- list_responses: GET /surveys/{survey_id}/responses
- get_survey_analytics: GET /surveys/{survey_id}/analytics
- list_action_plans: GET /surveys/{survey_id}/action-plans
- create_action_plan: POST /surveys/{survey_id}/action-plans
